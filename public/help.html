Staff Board quick help

Board

  • Use “Add staff” on each zone to assign team members.
  • The Undo button restores the last change you made.
  • Look for dashed cards that indicate empty zones.

Next Shift

  • Drag names into zones to stage the next shift.
  • Save the draft or publish when you are ready; Undo backs out the last change.

History

  • Pick a date or use quick filters to load saved history.
  • Export CSV to share assignments externally.
